About

Over 10 years in Linux-centric infrastructure and platform operations, with increasing depth in systems assurance, governance, and regulated environments. I work at the boundary between infrastructure reliability and organizational compliance: managing server hardware lifecycle, production platforms, backup/recovery, patch management, and controlled change routines while ensuring these operations produce audit-ready evidence.

My current focus is translating regulatory requirements (NIS2, GDPR, EU AI Act, DORA) into concrete technical controls, measurable signals, and verifiable evidence chains for infrastructure and AI workloads. I treat every system boundary as a contract: defined inputs, enforced invariants, observable behavior, and signed outputs.
