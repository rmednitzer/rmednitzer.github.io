About

Building production Linux platforms, Kubernetes clusters, and observability stacks since 2015. Now focused on the boundary where infrastructure meets compliance, safety, and AI.

Most compliance work happens after the fact: manual evidence collection, retroactive documentation, audit fire drills. I design systems where compliance evidence is a byproduct of normal operation — policy-as-code that enforces controls at runtime and produces signed audit trails automatically.
